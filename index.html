POYAH QR

Record-keeping with QR codes placed on collars or tags for vaccination, treatments, and health issues.

Mission

At POYAH QR, we help farmers keep track of their livestock's health using easy-to-scan QR codes. Our goal is to make it simple for farmers to manage vaccinations, treatments, and health records.

Vision

We want to be the top choice for farmers worldwide, making livestock health records accessible and easy to use. Our dream is for every farmer to have the best tools to keep their animals healthy and productive.
